# Submit expenses with Copilot in Microsoft Teams

[!INCLUDE[prod_short](includes/prod_short.md)] includes an *Expense Agent Copilot* that lets you submit expenses, check statuses, and manage reports directly from Microsoft Teams. Instead of switching to a separate app, you can use natural language to handle your expenses in a chat conversation.

The Copilot suggests details based on your receipts, but you always review and edit the information before you confirm.

## To submit an expense with a receipt

1. In Microsoft Teams, open the **D365 Expense Agent** Copilot.
1. Type a request such as "Submit a new expense with a receipt" or select the conversation starter.
1. When prompted, upload a photo or file of your receipt (JPEG, PNG, or PDF, up to 10 MB).
1. Review the details that Copilot prepopulates from your receipt, such as the amount, date, and merchant.
1. Edit any fields that need correction.
1. Confirm the expense to submit it.

> [!TIP]
> You can also drag and drop a receipt image directly into the chat to start the process.

## To check expense or report status

1. Open the **D365 Expense Agent** Copilot in Teams.
1. Ask a question such as "What's the status of my latest expense report?" or "Show me my expenses from last week."
1. Review the status information that Copilot returns.

## What you can ask Copilot to do

The Expense Agent Copilot supports the following actions through natural language:

- Submit a new expense with a receipt
- Update an existing expense
- Delete an expense
- List your expense reports

Here are a few example prompts to get started:

- "Submit a new expense with a receipt"
- "Help me create a new expense report"
- "What's the status of my latest expense report?"

> [!NOTE]
> Copilot detects receipt details using AI, which means the suggestions might not always be exact. Always review the prepopulated fields and make corrections before you confirm.
