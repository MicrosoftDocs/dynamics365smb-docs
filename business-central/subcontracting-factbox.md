# Subcontracting Details FactBox

In the FactBox that you find within your purchase orders, you have insights into the most important subcontracting details related to the selected purchase line. See at a glance the number of production orders, transfer orders, production operations, production components, and subcontractor prices. Click on the number to access more detailed information.

Furthermore, you also have the option in the cards of "Production and Transfer Orders" to reach the linked documents via FactBoxes and get an overview of the status of the process.

**You can find the FactBoxes with "Subcontracting Details" here:**

+ Purchase Order
+ Production Order Routing
+ Production Cockpit Planning View
+ Transfer Order
