When you design or maintain production BOMs and routings, you might work with uncertified structures for extended periods. When you try to close an uncertified production BOM or version, [!INCLUDE [prod_short](../includes/prod_short.md)] shows a message such as:

- *The Production BOM has not been certified. Are you sure you want to exit?*

The notification is useful, but it can also become noise if it displays many times per day. If you're comfortable not being reminded that the BOM or routing isn't certified, you can turn off the notification. The setting applies only to you, and doesn't stop the notification from displaying for others. 

To turn off the notification:

1. Choose the **Settings** icon :::image type="content" source="../media/ui-experience/settings_icon_small.png" alt-text="Screenshot of the Settings icon.":::, and select **My Settings**.
1. Choose **My Notifications**.
1. Search for **“Warn about non-certified production BOMs and routings”**.
1. Clear the **Enabled** checkbox.
