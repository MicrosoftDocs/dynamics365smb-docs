The report includes different layouts for different use cases:
* An Excel layout with worksheets designed for print and for analysis. 
* A Word layout designed for print/PDF output. 

You can export either layout from the **Report Layouts**, edit it in Excel or Word to cater for your needs, and import it back to [!INCLUDE[prod_short](prod_short.md)]. To learn more, go to 
[Creating your own report layouts](../ui-get-started-layouts.md).
